Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to the game!

Rules

  • You can enter both Player1's and Player2's username
  • Each player marks a sign on the board
  • First player to score 3-signs-in-a-row (horizontally, vertically or diagonally), wins the game
  • If all cells on the board have been marked and no winner was found, it's considered a tie
  • You may restart the game by pressing the corresponding button
  • Have fun and good luck!
